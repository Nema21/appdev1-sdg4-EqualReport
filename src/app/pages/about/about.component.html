About LearnHub

Supporting SDG 4 — Quality Education

🌍 What is SDG 4?

Sustainable Development Goal 4 aims to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

📚 About This App

LearnHub is a simple web application that helps users discover educational books using the Open Library API. It allows users to explore learning resources and improve access to knowledge.

🎯 Our Goal

Our goal is to make learning resources more accessible and encourage continuous education for everyone.
