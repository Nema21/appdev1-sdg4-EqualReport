Supporting UN Sustainable Development Goal 4 — Quality Education

🌍 What is SDG 4?

Sustainable Development Goal 4 is part of the United Nations 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. It aims to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all people, regardless of gender, location, or economic status.

🎯 SDG 4 Targets

  • ✅ Ensure all girls and boys complete free, equitable, quality primary and secondary education
  • ✅ Ensure equal access to affordable vocational and technical education
  • ✅ Eliminate gender disparities and ensure equal access for the vulnerable
  • ✅ Ensure all youth and adults have relevant skills for employment and entrepreneurship
  • ✅ Substantially increase the number of youth and adults with literacy and numeracy skills

📚 About This App

LearnHub is a web application built with Angular that helps users discover educational books using the Open Library API. It serves as a free digital learning companion that supports SDG 4 by making knowledge more accessible and equitable.

Built as a group project for APPDEV1 at the University of Baguio (S.Y. 2025–2026), this app demonstrates Angular concepts including routing, services, signals, guards, and API integration.
